Vehicle Description

This alluring example of the brand new Audi A3 Sport is now available here from Blackburn Audi. It is finished in the attractive manhattan grey which is a metallic finish. Benefits from the classic Audi 1.0 litre petrol engine 30 TFSI and has a manual transmission to make driving as easy as possible. Complemented with the 17in 5 Parallel-Spoke Design alloy wheels what look fantastic out on the road, paired up with the bright LED Headlights with LED Daytime Running Lights, this provides excellent visibility in all conditions of the road, whilst the Rear Parking Sensors allow for easy and safe maneuvering into all of those tighter spots. The interior features the striking 3 Spoke Leather Steering Wheel that provides the premium looks as well as sturdiness when turning your car in the right direction, it also features the Front Sport Seats that enhance the interior style as well as ensure comfort on both long and short journeys. The Dual-Zone Electronic Climate Control lets you and your passenger in the front to easily adjust the controls to get that perfect temperature. The included Cruise Control is a great feature to use during those longer motorway drives whilst the Audi Pre Sense Front with Pedestrian Recognition is an incredible safety feature allowing the car to stop itself in case of detected danger.
